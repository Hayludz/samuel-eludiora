The story

I studied Medicine & Surgery for eight years at the College of Health Sciences, Obafemi Awolowo University — finishing in 2026 — and I qualified as a doctor.

Three years in, something unexpected happened. I fell in love with writing — and specifically with the puzzle of getting people to find what I wrote. That puzzle has a name: Search Engine Optimisation. I became fascinated that words, structured correctly, could quietly pull an audience out of the noise of the internet and deliver it to a single page.

A doctor’s instinct is to understand the whole mechanism, not just the symptom — so I went deeper than the words. I trained in the technical machinery that makes content actually work: SEO, AI automation, and web development. Today I build automated content systems with n8n, Make.com and Zapier, and I design and manage sites in WordPress and beyond — so the publishing never stops and nothing falls through the cracks.

Since then, I’ve helped coaches and brands across many industries become genuine authorities — driving audiences toward them and growing their revenue. I do it with a team, and a system I call the Thought Leadership Framework.
